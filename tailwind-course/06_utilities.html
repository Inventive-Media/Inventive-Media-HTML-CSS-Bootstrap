3. Spacing (Padding & Margin)

Shorthand: {property}{sides}-{size}

Properties

  • p = Padding
  • m = Margin

Sides

  • t = top
  • b = bottom
  • l = left
  • r = right
  • x = left + right
  • y = top + bottom

Scale (1 unit = 4px)

p-0

p-1 (4px)

p-2 (8px)

p-4 (16px)

p-6 (24px)

p-8 (32px)
